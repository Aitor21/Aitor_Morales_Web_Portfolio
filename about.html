02

PASSION

  • DESIGN

    I think design is like a "magic wand". If a service or product is magic to solve a problem, it is the role of design to act as an intermediary for users to master it. We strive every day to create a "magic wand" that matches the users needs or desires.

  • TECHNOLOGY

    Technology has the power to change the lives of the world and individuals, as Arthur C. Clarke wrote,"Any sufficiently advanced technology is indistinguishable from magic." I want to be a person who can always catch up with the latest technology and respond to changes. I think it is the mission of designers as well as engineers to promote innovation.

  • STORIES

    I love watching TV shows, movies, playing games and reading manga. Stories can get people's attention and make things easier to understand and remember. I believe that any type of designer should consume every type of media to expand their minds and find new angles on how to tell their story.
